Who are we?

Nor'Easter is a website designed to help East Coast skiiers find proper ski resorts and help them set foot towards new mountains and adventures! The website provides resort querying features such as a map with many popular ski resorts in the New England area, and a search page to help filter resorts based on your preferences.

And to help keep track of all the skiing resorts in mind, Nor'Easter has implemented a checklist which allows users to save certain mountains to their personalized checklist and then mark off whether or not they've gone to ski the resort.
